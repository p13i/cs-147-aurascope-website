The Inception

Our team, composed of two Stanford undergraduates in Symbolic Systems and a master's student in Computer Science, met in CS147: Introduction to Human Computer Interaction. We were all placed in the Art and Digital Media Studio. As longtime musicians and music lovers, we formed a group with the initial goal of creating a project that would cater towards musicians.
