Low Fidelity Prototyping

We created a Low Fidelity prototype of the app using Balsamiq, an online design tool that mimics the look and feel of paper prototypes. We then tested the prototype on three participants, recording the time, number of clicks and NASA TLX score for each task we asked them to complete. We used our results to implement several UI changes.
